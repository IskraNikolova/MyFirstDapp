"The Nest is a decentralized application for video streaming and peer-to-peer video sharing powered by IPFS hypermedia protocol."

Upload Panel

In order to upload a video to The Nest simply choose your Ethereum Address and select your video for upload.There is no limit on file size.





Watch Streamed Videos

The video streaming is free.If you would like to support the video uploader or download the content, simply use the "Tip & Unlock Content" Function under the video.
